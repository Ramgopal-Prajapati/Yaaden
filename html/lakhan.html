Lakhan Prajapati



Prince , nano , lakhan , bhut naam hai mere pass mere bhai ke , per maine kebhi in naam se use nhi bulaya . Mere or mer bhai ki bilkul bhi nhi banti hai . Bachpan se ham logo ke bech bhut ladai hoti thii. Phele mera bhai mere kafi respect kerta tha per ab to wo kese ke samne , kuch bhi bol deta hai per koi ne jesa bhi hai mera bhai hai . lakhan ki ik aadat bhut buri hai ki wo gusaa jaldi kerta hai or hemesa chedcheda reheta hai . Lakhan ko pata nhi kiya hua wo mummy se bhi gusee main baat kerta hai oe mujse to , kebhi uska bas nhi chelta nhi to wo mujhe marne lag jaye . Wo kehete hai na ik adki insan ko pura badal dete hai , i think lakhan ke sath bhi wo hi hua . by the way ladki se yaad aaaya , lakhan ye samjhta hai ki main uske or uske piyar ke bech main aata huu, per esa nhi hai even main to cahta huu ki lakhan ko uska piyar mile , jo mujhe nhi mila kam se kam wo use to mile , per lakhan ye kebhi nhi samjhega . even main to her time use support kerta huu ki mummy ko pata nhi chal jaye , kher mujhe kiya kerna wo uski personal life hai . per maine bhi ab bhut time se use baat kerna he chod diya , because use jo acha lagata hai kerta hai . Uski ik buri aadat ye bhi hai ki wo mere dosto ke samne mere burai kerta hai ki , ye insan ( means main) bhut kercha kerta hai , ese paiso ki imat nhi pata or bhi bhut kuch. per mujhe koi ferk nhi pedta , maine bhi use baat kerna chod diya hai . kebhi to use ehsas hoga ki main galat nhi thaa , or nhi bhi hoga to koi ni . per may bhi lakhan ko mere jesa bhai kebhi nhi milsakta , jo usi her baad per use derta hoo, derta nhi huu per ye sochta huu ki thik hai yaar mera bhai hai gusaa ker bhi liya to kiya hua. per ab wo her kese ke samne kuch bhi bol deta hai , gher i don't care about it . Kesa bhi hoon yaar akhir bhai hai mera , jo bhi hoo acha hai , acha ladka hai , i wish bhaganji mere bhai ko uske pasand ki her chej dena .
