Ramgopal Mere Bare main kiya sochta hai



Bhai tu ik acha dost hai , or ik acha sathi bhi . pata hai tere ik baat mujhe sebse achi lagati hai ki tu sebko sath laker chelta hai , sebko apna samjhta hai . matalab phele main tujhe itna acha nhi samjhta tha per ab pata chela ki college main sebse acha insan tu he hai , thoda chutiya lagata hai per dil ka bada he acha hai. Sachi yaar bhai tu mera acha dost hai , or hemesha regega , pata hai main tujhe laker bas ye hi sochta huu ki jarur kuch bada kerega or ha bhai tu risk lene wala banda hai. yaar sp tu hamesha esa he rehena bhai sabke sath ache se . pata hai college main jab first time mere ladai hui thi to tu mere sath kada thaa wo baat main kebhi nhi bhulunga thanks yaar . main tujhe or rashi ko laker bada he pereshan reheta huu ki tum sath main kiyon nhi reh sakte yaar rashi tujhse bhut piyar kerti hai , i know ki tu phelese he kese or se piyar kerta hai per yaar rashi ka kiya wo bhi to tujhe piyar kerti hai na . kher chod wo tu samjh lena apne hesab se . per mere liye to tu acha insan hai yaar bhai sach main . i wish ke tere jesa friend her kee ko mile taki her koi proud feel kere . or ha ik baat or tere bare main sale tujhe dekhker main gali dena shikh gya huu, pata nhi tere sakal dekhker mujhe gali aati hai , mujhe begadne wala tu or lokesh he bhai , tum per case kerunga main , ki sahab in dono ne milker mujhe bigada hai .
