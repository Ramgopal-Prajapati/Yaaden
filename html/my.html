Main Ramgopal prajapati, Main ik simple sa ladka hu , jisne life main bhut kuch kerne ka soch hai . Ik chote se Family ka ladka hu , maine apni life main bachpan se he bhut problem face ki hai , per mere mummy ji ne hamara her kadam per sath diya hai . Hamare papa , jam mera birth hua tafhi mummy ko chodker chelegy the kese dusri ladki ko lekar , hame mummy ji ne se samhala or bada kiya or bhut sare sapne dekhaye . Maine apni life main bhut fun bhi kiya or bhut pereshan bhi huaa. Maine apni 12th class tak ki pedai government school main puri ki bas 9th or 10th private school main pada thaa. Main ik ladki se bhut piyar kerta huu , mainre life main mujhe sebse jada sath mummy or rukhmaji jese main piyar kerta huu unka hi mila hai or uske bad mere dost bhi hai. Main apni higher education ke liye Indore aaya huu or Engineering ker rha huu . Bhut sare sapne hai bhut kuch kerna hai life main . Main kebe kese ke bare main bura nhi sochta hemesha try kerta huu ki sebke sath ache se rhu , per khi bar ye nhi hoo pata hai . Main phele bhut expectations rekhta thaa per ab main kese se kuch bhi expectation nhi rekhta bas apne kam main mast rho . Main ik acha dost hu , ik acha beta hu , or ik acha boyfriend bhi thaa . Main sebse jada piyar apni mummy ji , rukhmaji or apne dosto ko kerta huu. Mera ik bhai bhi hai lakhan ham dono twince brother hai , per hamare bilkul bhi nhi banti hai , ham log sath rehete hai per khi bar hame baat kiye mahino hoo jate hai . Maine kudh se wada kiya hai ki main life main kuch acha kerunga .Hene ko to mainre bare main or bhi bhut hai per itna kafi hai mere bare main jo mujhe describe kerta hai . Overal main ik acha ladka huu
