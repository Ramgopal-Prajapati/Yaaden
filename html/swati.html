Ramgopal Mere Bare main kiya sochta hai



Swati ab aapke bare main kiya he batao yaar , mere life ki sebse badi galti aap thee , per main janta nhi thaa ki aap ese ladki hoo . sach main yaar main aapko achi ladki manta thaa per aap to advance nikli or kher chodo mujhe kiya he kerna hai . per aapke sath kiss kiya or jo bhi kiya wo acha thaa , sachi main maine socha nhi thaa ki aapke sath ye seb bhi hoga . mere liye wo time bhut acha thaa . or fhir aapne apki asliyat dikhai or seb end . kher , jo bhi thaa phela aap bhut ache thee , phir app bhut he jaldi badal gye .
