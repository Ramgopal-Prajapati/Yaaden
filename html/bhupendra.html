Haaaa, Mere jaan hai bhopu. samje nhi , samjata huu bhupendra ka naam bhopu hai , jise ham sebne bede piyar se rekha hia "Bhopu". Ye banda jab tak roj college main mujhe chata nhi marta hai jab tak ese chen nhi milta . sala mood kesa bhi hoo is bande se milker seb thik hoo jata hai . phele main ese bada hi simple sakajhta thaa per ye to kamina nikla yaar . mere bhut piyara dost hai yaar. Ye mera college ka dost hai college main pheli friendship bhupendra or lokesh ke sath hi hui thii, kebhi nhi socha thaa ki ye friendship itni paki hoojayegi , Hamare group ka mast banda hai yaar bhopu. Matalab aapka mood kesa bhi hoo ise koi ferk nhi pedta iska mood hemesha mast reheta hai , or ye hemesha piyaar se mujhe " I Love mere Jaan " Kheta hai , isko laker mujhe kebhi kebhi thodi chinta hoti hai ki ye kese fight kerega , per ker leta hai banda . Iska sapna IAS banana hai , or jis din ye IAS bana sebse phele main bus-station per jaker ladai kerunga or ager police aai to ise call kerunga or ye police ki ese ki tese ker dega . main bhagwanji se bhut khush huu ki unhone mujhe bhopu se milaya . Pata nhi college ke bad kiya hoga per bhopu mere acho dost rehega hemesha . Ham sath main masti kerte hai , bada hi maja aata hai . Bhopu mere liye bhai jesa hai . Per kamina hai sala, Kebhi kesa ko bura nhi sochta . mast ladaka hia yaar . bas ab jada tarifh hoogye mujhe itne he paise mile the ab ise jada tafir kerwani hoo to or paise de mujhe phele , ha bhopu tujhe hi bol raha huu.
