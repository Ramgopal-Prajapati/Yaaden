Ye hi Atul Mera dost



Atul matalab na kuch kerta hai or na hame kerne deta hai. sala puri school life main na kese ladki ko patane diya or na khud pataya , halaki maine jo mujhe kerna thaa wo kiya , per atul ik dam shrifh ladaka hai esa sebko lagata hai , per uski asliyat main or arvin jante hai bas . Pedne mian acha hai , hamari dosti 10th class main hui thi or Polay main mera phela dost thaa atul , jise dosti itni paki hoo gye ki aaj wo mera best friend hai. Matalab polay main mera sebse acha dost atul or arvin he hai bas . Atul ki bhan main mujhe rakhi bandi hai or atul ki mummy mujhe apna beda manti hai , bhut achi hai atul ki mummy mujmain or atul main kebhi ferka nhi kerti . Or atul ik namber ka gada hai, apne bade papa se bhut derta hai , main or arvin or ishwer milker atul ki bhut tang khicte hai , or jab atul pereshan hota hai to hame bada maja aata hai . Per yaar atul hai acha ladaka , sach main dosti kese hete hia atul se pucho . Yaar pata nhi mere dosti hemesa se ache insan se hi hoti hai. Mere najar main atul acha ladaka hai , or mera acha dost bhi , Atul photographer banna chata hai i wish ki wo apna sapna pura kere .
